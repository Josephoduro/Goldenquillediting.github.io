Biography

Gift Gugu Mona is a South African born poet, philosopher, songwriter and philanthropist.
She is an internationally acclaimed writer who holds a Doctor of Philosophy Degree. Her captivating writings have been cited in publications and platforms worldwide. Dr Mona’s work continues to make a significant impact,earning her global recognition as one of Africa’s sought after transformational writers. Through her creative work, she fulfils her God-given mandate and passion as a Transformational Coach. Her enthusiasm for humanity, history, culture and her continent is visible in her books. The bulk of her writings is inspired by Divine wisdom and ancient African storytelling practices. She also draws inspiration from those who provide unwavering support for her work.
