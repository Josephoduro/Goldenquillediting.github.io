Our Privacy Policy

At Golden Quill Editing Solutions, we take the privacy and security of our customers' personal and confidential information very seriously., Jan 13, 2023.

At Golden Quill Editing Solutions, we take the privacy and security of our customers' personal and confidential information very seriously. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use and protect it, and your rights as a customer.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information from customers who use our editing services, such as name, email address, phone number, and payment information. We also collect information about the documents and files submitted for editing, such as their content and format.

Use of Information

We use the personal information we collect to provide and improve our editing services, communicate with customers, and process payments. We use the information about the documents submitted for editing to complete the requested services and provide feedback and suggestions. We do not share personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

Security Measures

We take appropriate security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use industry-standard encryption and authentication methods to protect the confidentiality and integrity of customer information.

Retention of Information

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide editing services and comply with legal and regulatory requirements. After this time, we securely delete or anonymize the information.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You also have the right to withdraw your consent for the collection and use of your information, and to object to the processing of your information.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify customers of any material changes and provide an updated policy on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or practices, please contact us at goldenquillep@gmail.com.
